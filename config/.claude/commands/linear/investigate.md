## Your Task

Your job is to investigate Linear ticket EM1-$ARGUMENTS.

1. **Review the ticket** — Read the ticket details and all comments for full context on what is being reported or requested.
2. **Investigate** — Explore the codebase to build an understanding of the relevant code and surrounding context.
   - For **bug tickets**: identify the root cause and any related failure points.
   - For **feature/task tickets**: identify where changes would need to be made, what existing patterns to follow, and any constraints or risks.
3. **Post your findings** — Add a comment on the ticket summarizing:
   - What you found during your investigation
   - Key considerations, risks, or dependencies
   - A recommended approach for implementation
